WHO WE ARE

Arete International Pvt. Ltd. is a privately traded company established in 2020. This Company is incorporated under the Companies Act, and our registered office is at Tangal 5, Kathmandu.

We started off with a little question, glowing at the breakfast table, "why can't Nepal have the best the world has to offer in sustainable mobility?". The question, whilst seemingly simple, did not have an answer in 2020, and even if there were an answer, it wouldn't be 'simple'; Otherwise, it'd already be done.

Being a newly incorporated company, we started with the one brand our head turned towards when we thought,"well, what is the best the world has to offer in sustainable mobility?" : Tesla.

Arete International is the first distributor of Tesla vehicles in Nepal, it is only one of many brands that we aim to add to our portfolio in order to make transit in Nepal truly sustainable, for every one of its residents.

OUR VISION

Our Vision is to provide a sustainable option to every resident of Nepal to commute with. We began with bringing Tesla to the top of the world, but we are aware of the small demographic this will aid. Arete International envisions a future where transit can be harmless to mother nature and to users' pockets. Through projects on mass transit and cost-friendly alternatives, we aim to create a greener, cleaner Nepal.

OUR MISSION

Our mission now is to provide reliable, established solutions found all over the world to Nepal. To offer our customers a unique buying experience that matches the commitment and quality shown around the planet. To ensure an experience that truly creates a relationship with a customer.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Arete International has been working with Hoste Hainse to support the education of underprivileged children in the poorest regions of Nepal. Each child gets sponsored for eleven years on the occasion of a purchase of a vehicle.

We believe that education should be a right for each child, not an opportunity, and through CSR partnerships, we inch closer to breaking every poverty cycle in the country.
