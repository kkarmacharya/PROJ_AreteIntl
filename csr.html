Hoste Hainse

Arete International has been working with Hoste Hainse to support the education of underprivileged children in the poorest regions of Nepal. Each child gets sponsored for eleven years on the occasion of a purchase of a vehicle.

We believe that education should be a right for each child, not an opportunity, and through CSR partnerships, we inch closer to breaking every poverty cycle in the country.

Food for Life Nepal

Arete International has been working with Food for Life Nepal. To ensure that no child in Nepal is deprived of education because of hunger. Nobody should have to pick between studying or starving.

With every vehicle sold, we provide 5000 meals to children at the Food For Life community schools.

By partnering with Food for Life Nepal, we are proud to create a positive impact on those in our community. To ensure that malnutrition among children is kept low and enrollment at schools keeps increasing. Whilst also making sure to give students the best shot at academic success and reduce their dependencies on cheap, unhealthy junk food.
