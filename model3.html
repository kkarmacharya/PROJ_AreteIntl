Safety

Built for Safety

Model 3 achieved a Euro NCAP 5-star safety rating overall and in every category. It also earned the highest score awarded for the Safety Assist category through its advanced active safety features including a suite of Lane Support Systems, Seatbelt Reminder, Speed Assist, and Automatic Emergency Braking.

5-Star Rating

Model 3 achieved NHTSA 5-star safety ratings in every category and subcategory.

Top Safety Pick+

Model 3 received the IIHS Top Safety Pick+ award, with top ratings in all crashworthiness and front crash prevention categories.

Safety First Design

Model 3 is built from the ground up as an electric vehicle—with ultra high-strength steel and a low, solid center of gravity.

Rigid Structure

A material mix of aluminum and steel help to achieve the best structural rigidity, and increased occupant safety.

Impact Protection

Combined with impact-absorbing rails and center pillar reinforcements, the strength and support of the rigid battery pack provides protection from every side.

Very Low Rollover Risk

The position and weight of the floor-mounted battery pack provides a very low center of gravity—allowing for a very low rollover risk.

Performance

Quickest Acceleration

An evolution in automobile engineering

Tesla’s advanced electric powertrain delivers exhilarating performance. Unlike a gasoline internal combustion engine with hundreds of moving parts, Tesla electric motors have only one moving piece: the rotor. As a result, Model 3 acceleration is instantaneous, silent and smooth. Step on the accelerator and in as little as 5.4 seconds Model 3 is travelling 100 km per hour, without hesitation, and without a drop of gasoline. Tesla's are an evolution in automobile engineering.

Range

Model 3 is fully electric, so you never need to visit a gas station again. If you charge overnight at home, you can wake up to a full battery every morning. And when you’re on the road, it’s easy to plug in along the way—at any public station.
